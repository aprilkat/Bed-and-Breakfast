Come stay with us!

Kailua Kove is one of Oahu's last legally licensed B&B and it is waiting for you!
We are just a five minute walk from the beach. Our local beach is pretty quite. The locals enjoy taking their daily walks or walking their dogs there. The beach is very easy to locate. Turn left and walk past two buildings and the new restaurant, Nico's Kailua. Then cross the bridge and after the first street to your left you will find a narrow path that leads to the beach.

Our Rooms

As you walk into our rooms, the first thing that you will notice is how fresh, airy, and up-to-date they are. The kitchenette has everything you need. The kitchenette is equiped with granite countertops, a sink, a mini-fridge, a keurig coffeemaker, a toaster, and a microwave. Kitchen utensils are provided. A daily self-serve continental breakfast is included. We re-stock it daily for you to enjoy at your leisure.
